What is Medico

Founded in 2018, MedicoDenta Company is based in the State of Kuwait. Our Focus is on the distribution of the leading international brands of dental products. We provide a wide range of products and services catering for the Dental profession and industry. Our success is primarily due to our great reputable customer service and our high quality products.

Vision

Our vision is to surpass our customers expectations and needs through our excellent services, our cutting edge and high quality products provided by our multinational suppliers and partners. With our tactics and strategy we will improve and expand to be one of the top distributors in the local and regional market.

Mission

Provide high quality, affordable product, Create and cultivate long term relationships with our customers, respond immediately or very shortly to the requests of our customers,achieve complete customer satisfaction,Improve our services continuously,Maintain professional relationships with our loyal and dedicated customers and suppliers by ongoing communication to achieve the highest standard of performance and services.

Goal

At MedicoDenta, our main goal is to serve all the specialized and diverse demands of the dental community while conducting ourselves with honesty and integrity. We dedicate our efforts to our dear customers and suppliers.
